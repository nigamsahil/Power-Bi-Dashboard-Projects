# Credit Card Transaction & Customer Analysis Dashboard

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze **credit card transactions** and **customer behavior**. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, customer segments, card usage, expenditure patterns, and overall business performance.

### 1. **Credit Card Transaction Report**
This report includes insights such as:
- Quarterly Revenue & Transaction Count
- Revenue by Expenditure Categories (Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, Grocery, Food, Travel)
- Revenue by Customer Education Level
- Revenue by Customer Job
- Customer Acquisition Cost by Card Category (Blue, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- Transaction Type Analysis (Swipe, Chip, Online)
- Card Category Performance Table
- **KPIs**
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Interest Earned
  - Total Transaction Count
  - Total Transaction Amount

---

### 2. **Credit Card Customer Report**
Contains customer-level insights such as:
- Revenue by Income Group (Low, Medium, High)
- Revenue by Customer Job Category
- Weekly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Age Group
- Revenue by Dependents
- Revenue by Education Level
- Top 5 States by Revenue (TX, NY, CA, FL, NJ)
- Revenue by Marital Status
